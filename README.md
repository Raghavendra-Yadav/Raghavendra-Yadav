## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm an AI Engineer and Full-Stack Developer with 2+ years of production experience, now going deep on LLM systems, RAG architectures, and autonomous agents. I build things that ship — then I document the process publicly so others can skip the painful parts. You can follow the journey on YouTube, read the breakdowns on Medium, and catch the behind-the-scenes on Instagram ([@GOLLA_RAGHAVENDRA_YADAV](https://www.instagram.com/GOLLA_RAGHAVENDRA_YADAV/)).

- 🔭 **Currently Building:** NORD Life OS (an end-of-day personal OS) & a MongoDB-backed Project Tracking System — both being pivoted into AI Engineering proof-of-work
- 🌱 **Currently Learning:** RAG architectures, AI agents, LLM fine-tuning, and production-grade ML systems
- 💬 **Ask Me About:** LangChain, FastAPI, RAG pipelines, full-stack development with Next.js, and how to transition from web dev into AI Engineering
- ⚡ **Fun Fact:** I moved from India to Bridgeport, CT — and I'm shipping production AI systems before most people finish their morning coffee
